# COMPLETE SUBMISSION GUIDE

This guide will provide you with comprehensive step-by-step instructions for completing the submission process for both the iOS App Store and Google Play Store.

## 1. **Creating Developer Accounts**

### iOS App Store:
- Visit [Apple Developer](https://developer.apple.com/programs/) and click on "Enroll".
- Sign in with your Apple ID or create a new one.
- Select the appropriate account type (Individual or Organization).
- Review and agree to the terms.
- Pay the annual fee of $99.
- Complete the registration process.

### Google Play Store:
- Go to the [Google Play Console](https://play.google.com/console) and sign in with your Google account.
- Accept the Developer Agreement.
- Pay the one-time registration fee of $25.
- Fill out the required details like Developer name and email.

## 2. **Generating Builds**

### For iOS:
- Open your project in Xcode.
- Configure your app’s settings (App ID, provisioning profile).
- Select the target device (iPhone or iPad).
- Go to **Product** > **Archive** to create a build.
- After successful archiving, click **Distribute App** to upload to the App Store.

### For Android:
- Open your Android project in Android Studio.
- Navigate to **Build** > **Generate Signed Bundle / APK**.
- Choose between Android App Bundle or APK.
- Fill out the signing information and finish the wizard to generate the build.

## 3. **Creating App Listings**

### iOS App Store:
- Log in to App Store Connect at [App Store Connect](https://appstoreconnect.apple.com).
- Click on **My Apps** and then the + button to create a new app.
- Fill in the necessary information including app name, description, keywords, and more.

### Google Play Store:
- Log in to your Google Play Console.
- Click on **All Apps** > **Create App**.
- Provide the app details like title, description, and category.

## 4. **Uploading Screenshots**

### iOS App Store:
- In your app listing on App Store Connect, scroll to the **Screenshots** section.
- Upload the required screenshots for each device size supporting your app.
- Make sure to follow Apple’s guidelines for screenshot dimensions.

### Google Play Store:
- In your app listing on Google Play Console, find the **Graphics** section.
- Upload your feature graphic, screenshots, and promotional graphics as required.

## 5. **Submitting for Review**

### iOS App Store:
- After all required information is filled out, click on the **Submit for Review** button in App Store Connect.
- Wait for Apple’s review process, which may take a few days.

### Google Play Store:
- Once your app is ready and all details are provided, click **Release to Production** in the Google Play Console.
- Monitor the release status and respond to any feedback from the review process.

---

Following these steps will help ensure a smooth submission process for both the iOS App Store and Google Play Store. Good luck!
