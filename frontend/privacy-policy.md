# Privacy Policy for Optical Rx Now

**Last Updated:** February 3, 2026

## Overview
Optical Rx Now is a mobile app designed to help you store and manage optical prescriptions for yourself and your family. All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal information.

## Information We Store Locally

### Family Member Names
- Names you enter to organize prescriptions
- Stored locally on your device only
- Not transmitted to any servers

### Prescription Images
- Photos of optical prescriptions you capture or upload
- Stored locally on your device as base64-encoded data
- Not transmitted to any servers

### Device Permissions

#### Camera
- Used to capture photos of prescriptions
- Images remain on your device

#### Photo Library
- Used to select existing prescription photos
- Selected images remain on your device

#### Location (Optional)
- Used to find nearby optical stores
- Location data is not stored or transmitted
- Used only when you actively search for stores

## Data Storage
All data is stored locally using AsyncStorage on your device. We do not maintain servers, databases, or any backend infrastructure to collect your information.

## Data Sharing
We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to third parties. All information remains on your device.

## Data Security
- Data is stored locally on your device
- No transmission to external servers
- Your device's built-in security protects your data

## Children's Privacy
We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app stores prescription data locally for users of all ages.

## Your Rights
You can delete all app data at any time by:
- Deleting individual prescriptions within the app
- Uninstalling the app to remove all stored data

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Third-Party Services
This app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

This app complies with:
- Apple App Store Guidelines
- Google Play Store Policies
- COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- General data protection best practices
