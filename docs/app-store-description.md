## App Store Questions & Answers

### Age Rating
**Age Rating:** 4+ (iOS) / Everyone (Android)
**Age Verification:** Required (18+) on first launch

### Privacy Questions

**Does this app collect user data?**
No. All data is stored locally on the user's device. No data is transmitted to servers or third parties.

**Does this app use third-party analytics?**
No. We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

**Does this app require an internet connection?**
No. The app works completely offline. Internet is only needed for optional features like finding nearby stores.

**Does this app comply with HIPAA?**
The app is not a HIPAA-covered entity because it does not transmit health information. All data stays on the user's device.

### Content Questions

**Is this app a medical device?**
No. This app is for storing and organizing prescription information only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

**Do I need to create an account?**
No. The app requires no login or account creation. All data is stored locally on your device.

**Can I use this app for commercial purposes?**
The app is designed for personal use to manage family prescriptions. Commercial use is not supported.
