Privacy Policy for Optical Rx Now

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

Overview

Optical Rx Now is a mobile app designed to help you store and manage optical prescriptions for yourself and your family. All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share your personal information.

Information We Store Locally

Prescription Data

  • Eyeglass Prescriptions: Optical prescription details (sphere, cylinder, axis, pupillary distance, etc.)
  • Contact Lens Prescriptions: Contact lens specifications and parameters
  • Prescription Images: Photos of physical prescription documents
  • Stored Locally: All prescription data is stored on your device using AsyncStorage
  • Not Transmitted: No prescription data is sent to external servers or third parties

Family Member Names

  • Names you enter to organize prescriptions
  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Not transmitted to any servers

Prescription Images

  • Photos of optical prescriptions you capture or upload
  • Stored locally on your device as base64-encoded data
  • Not transmitted to any servers

Data Retention Policies

Local Storage

  • Retention Period: Data is retained indefinitely on your device until you delete it
  • User Control: You have complete control over your data at all times
  • Deletion: You can delete individual prescriptions or family members within the app
  • Complete Removal: Uninstalling the app removes all stored data from your device

No Server Storage

  • We do not maintain servers or databases
  • No backup copies are created by us
  • All data remains exclusively on your device

Device Permissions

Camera

  • Purpose: Capture photos of prescription documents
  • Usage: Only activated when you choose to take a photo
  • Storage: Images remain on your device
  • Privacy: Photos are not transmitted or shared

Photo Library

  • Purpose: Select existing prescription photos from your device
  • Usage: Only activated when you choose to import a photo
  • Storage: Selected images remain on your device
  • Privacy: Photos are not transmitted or shared

Location (Optional)

  • Purpose: Find nearby optical stores (e.g., Sam's Club, LensCrafters)
  • Usage: Only when you actively search for stores
  • Storage: Location data is not stored or transmitted
  • Privacy: Used momentarily for search, then discarded

Data Security

Local Security Measures

  • Data is stored locally on your device using encrypted AsyncStorage
  • Your device's built-in security (PIN, biometrics) protects your data
  • No transmission to external servers eliminates network security risks
  • App uses secure storage practices recommended by Apple and Google

No Cloud Storage

  • No data synchronization or cloud backup
  • No server-side vulnerabilities
  • Complete offline functionality

Data Sharing

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit your data to third parties because:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We have no servers or databases
  • We have no advertising partners
  • We have no analytics services

No Tracking

  • We do not track user behavior
  • We do not use analytics tools
  • We do not collect usage statistics
  • We do not use advertising identifiers

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Age Requirements

  • The app is designed for users of all ages
  • Parents can store prescriptions for their children
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children because all data is stored locally on the device

Parental Control

  • Parents have full control over data for family members
  • Parents can add, view, edit, and delete prescriptions for children
  • No data is transmitted from the device

COPPA Compliance

This app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) because:

  • We do not collect personal information from children
  • All data is stored locally on the user's device
  • No information is transmitted to servers or third parties
  • Parents maintain complete control over all data

State-Specific Privacy Requirements

Your California Privacy Rights:

  • Right to Know: You can view all data stored in the app at any time
  • Right to Delete: You can delete your data within the app or by uninstalling
  • Right to Opt-Out: Not applicable - we don't sell or share data
  • No Sale of Personal Information: We do not sell personal information
  • Do Not Track: We do not track users across apps or websites

Other States

We comply with privacy laws in all U.S. states, including:

  • Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA)
  • Colorado Privacy Act (CPA)
  • Connecticut Data Privacy Act (CTDPA)
  • Utah Consumer Privacy Act (UCPA)

Compliance Method: Since all data is stored locally and never transmitted, state privacy requirements are automatically satisfied.

Prescription Data Handling

Types of Prescription Data Stored

  1. Eyeglass Prescriptions:
    • Sphere, Cylinder, Axis values
    • Pupillary Distance (PD)
    • Prism and Base (if applicable)
    • Doctor information
    • Prescription date and expiration
  2. Contact Lens Prescriptions:
    • Base Curve, Diameter
    • Power/Sphere
    • Brand and type
    • Doctor information
    • Prescription date and expiration
  3. Prescription Images:
    • Photographs of paper prescriptions
    • Stored as base64-encoded images
    • Associated with prescription records

Medical Data Privacy

  • HIPAA Status: This app is not a HIPAA-covered entity as it does not transmit health information
  • Local Storage Only: Prescription data never leaves your device
  • User Responsibility: Users are responsible for protecting their device with passwords/biometrics
  • No Medical Advice: The app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment

Prescription Accuracy

  • Users are responsible for accurately entering prescription data
  • The app is a storage tool only, not a medical device
  • Always consult with a qualified eye care professional for medical advice

International Privacy Compliance

  • Data Minimization: Only stores data you explicitly enter
  • Purpose Limitation: Data used only for prescription storage
  • Storage Limitation: Data retained only on your device
  • Right to Access: You can view all your data in the app
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete data within the app
  • Right to Data Portability: You can export prescriptions (print/share)

Other International Laws

The app's local-only storage model ensures compliance with privacy laws worldwide.
